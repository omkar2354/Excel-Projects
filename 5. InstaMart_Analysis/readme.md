## 📘 Project Overview
The **Instamart Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an **Excel-based analytical project** designed to evaluate Instamart’s **sales performance**, **customer satisfaction**, and **inventory distribution**.  
It aims to help identify **key business insights**, **sales trends**, and **optimization opportunities** through interactive Excel visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Business Requirement
To perform a comprehensive analysis of Instamart’s overall performance using Excel by exploring relationships between **fat content**, **item types**, **outlet characteristics**, and **sales metrics**.  
The analysis helps in understanding what drives total sales and customer satisfaction.

---

## 📊 KPI Requirements

| **KPI Name** | **Description** |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Total Sales** | The overall revenue generated from all items sold. |
| **Average Sales** | The average revenue per sale. |
| **Number of Items** | Total count of unique items sold. |
| **Average Rating** | The average customer satisfaction score. |

These KPIs form the foundation of the dashboard and are displayed at the top of the report.

---

## 📈 Chart Requirements

| **No.** | **Chart Title** | **Objective** | **Chart Type** |
|----------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Total Sales by Fat Content** | Analyze the impact of fat content (Low Fat vs Regular) on total sales. | Donut/Pie Chart |
| 2 | **Total Sales by Item Type** | Identify the performance of different item types in total sales. | Bar Chart |
| 3 | **Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales** | Compare total sales across different outlets segmented by fat content. | Stacked Column Chart |
| 4 | **Total Sales by Outlet Establishment** | Evaluate how outlet age or type influences sales. | Line Chart |
| 5 | **Sales by Outlet Size** | Analyze correlation between outlet size and total sales. | Donut / Pie Chart |
| 6 | **Sales by Outlet Location** | Assess geographic sales distribution across different locations (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3). | Funnel Chart |
| 7 | **All Metrics by Outlet Type** | Provide a combined view of all KPIs (Total Sales, Avg Sales, Number of Items, Avg Rating). | Matrix Table / Card Layout |

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

- **Tier 3 outlets** generated the **highest total sales**, followed by Tier 2.  
- **Regular fat products** contributed more to total revenue than low-fat items.  
- **Fruits & Vegetables** and **Snack Foods** were the top-performing categories.  
- **Supermarket Type 1** outlets dominated in total sales and number of items sold.  
- Older outlet establishments maintained **consistent performance** over the years.

## 🧰 Tools & Techniques Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables for summarizing data  
  - Pivot Charts for visualization  
  - Data Validation & Slicers for interactive filtering  
  - Conditional Formatting for visual emphasis  
  - Formulas (SUMIFS, AVERAGEIFS, COUNTIFS) for KPI calculations

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open **Instamart_Project.xlsx** in Microsoft Excel (2016 or later).  
2. Go to the **Dashboard** sheet.  
3. Use **Slicers** (filters) for Outlet Size, Location, or Item Type to explore insights dynamically.  
4. Review KPI cards and charts to interpret performance.
